India -Rising of Bharat

  • India's GDP will rise by USD 406 billion by 2050 and more than 43 million jobs will be created, as the Asia's third-largest economy leaps towards a net-zero target, according to a report by Observer Research Foundation (ORF)
  • India is playing an increasingly important role in the global economy and, correspondingly, in resource use and emissions.
  • Fuelled by the ambitions and skills of an large, young and educated population, it will aspire for a more prominent place in the globalizing world of the 21st century. Official projections expect a slowly stabilizing population and a sustained high rate of economic growth for the next three to five decades, with a significant increase in the average level of the standard of living.
